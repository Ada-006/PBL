Abstract

This system demonstrates an integrated artificial intelligence framework for agricultural decision support. The platform combines convolutional neural networks for disease detection, vegetation index analysis for crop monitoring, and time-series forecasting for agricultural market prediction. The objective is to enhance productivity, sustainability, and profitability through data-driven insights.

I. Crop Disease Detection Module

Select a leaf image to simulate CNN-based disease classification.

II. Crop Monitoring and Growth Analysis

Simulated vegetation index (NDVI) and crop health evaluation.

III. Market Trend Prediction Module

Select crop commodity for price forecasting.

IV. Integrated Recommendation Report
